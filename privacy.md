# Web Jotter Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 23, 2025**

Web Jotter is a local-first browser extension that helps you save and restore tab sessions and capture text highlights with source links. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data stays on your device.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Web Jotter does **not collect, transmit, sell, or share** any personal information.

- All sessions, highlights, settings, and preferences are stored **locally on your device** using Chrome’s `chrome.storage.local` API.
- Web Jotter does **not** create accounts, use cloud storage, track behavior, or send any data to external servers.
- No analytics, crash reports, or background data collection are performed.

---

## 2. Data Access & Processing

Web Jotter only processes the information required for its core features, and **only when you explicitly interact with the extension**.

### Website Content (User-Selected Text)
When you right-click selected text and choose **“Save to Web Jotter Highlights,”** Web Jotter saves:
- The text you selected  
- The URL of the page it came from  

This data is stored **only on your device**.

### Session Data
When you press **“Save Tabs as Session,”** Web Jotter stores:
- The URLs and titles of the tabs in your current window  

This data is stored **locally** and **only when you intentionally save a session**.

Web Jotter does **not** access your browsing history or monitor your activity on webpages.

---

## 3. Permissions

Web Jotter requires the following Chrome permissions:

- **Tabs**  
  Allows Web Jotter to read open tab titles and URLs *only when you save or restore a session*.

- **Storage**  
  Used for storing sessions, highlights, and settings on your device.

- **Context Menus**  
  Required to show the **“Save to Web Jotter Highlights”** option when you right-click selected text.

- **Host Permissions (`<all_urls>`)**  
  Required so Web Jotter can read the URL of the page you choose to save a highlight from.  
  Web Jotter does **not** scan pages or read content unless you explicitly save something.

---

## 4. Optional Future Features

Features such as optional cloud sync or multi-device access may be added in the future.  
If introduced, these features will be:

- **Fully opt-in**  
- **Disabled by default**  
- **Documented in an updated Privacy Policy**

No data will ever be transmitted without your explicit consent.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Web Jotter does not collect personal data and is safe for all ages.  
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
